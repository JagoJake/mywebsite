About Me

My previous career as a professional chef of eight years was abruptly put on hold in early 2020 due to the situation we found ourselves in globally, out of necessity, I, like so many others had to pivot direction so I joined BRS1, Amazon’s large fulfilment centre in Bristol U.K.

Although I didn’t realise it at the time this was truly a blessing in disguise and has been a great opportunity to hone existing skills and develop many more.

Through hard work and determination, I have grown into a leadership position within the company, monitoring operations as an FC team lead overseeing a department of 70+ Amazon Associates.

However, by far the most positive thing to come from this change is that I have been able to make use of Amazon’s career choice, an amazing program designed to assist with employee tuition, granting the ability to continue working whilst also studying, gaining knowledge and technical skills in a wide range of sectors, in my case the exciting world of software development.
